## 🎯 Course Description

CS50x is Harvard University's introduction to the intellectual enterprises of computer science and the art of programming. This course teaches students how to think algorithmically and solve problems efficiently. Designed for both beginners and experienced programmers, with two-thirds of students having no prior programming experience.

### Core Topics Covered
- **Computational thinking** and problem-solving strategies
- **Abstraction** and algorithmic design
- **Data structures** and their applications
- **Memory management** and resource optimization
- **Software engineering** principles
- **Security** fundamentals
- **Web programming** and development
