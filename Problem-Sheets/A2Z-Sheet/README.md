## 🎯 How to Approach

### Recommended Strategy

1. **Follow Sequential Order** - The steps are carefully ordered for optimal learning
2. **Complete Each Step** - Don't skip ahead; each step builds on previous ones
3. **Watch Video Lectures** - Striver's YouTube channel has detailed explanations
4. **Code Along** - Implement solutions yourself before checking answers
5. **Make Notes** - Document patterns and approaches you learn
6. **Revise Regularly** - Revisit completed sections periodically

### Daily Schedule Suggestions

**Beginners (3-6 months)**:
- 2-3 problems per day
- 1-2 hours of focused study
- Watch related video lectures

**Intermediate (2-3 months)**:
- 3-5 problems per day
- 2-3 hours daily
- Quick reviews of concepts

**Advanced (1-2 months - revision)**:
- 5-7 problems per day
- Focus on weak areas
- Timed practice
